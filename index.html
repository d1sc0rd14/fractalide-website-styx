Vision

Fractalide aims to be a free and open rapid application development (RAD) platform with integrated marketplace, allowing one to purchase reuseable and reproducible applications. The RAD platform is based on Hypercard which allows non-programmers to mix and match components created by programmers thus creating new applications. The RAD platform will then be extended to support ETCDEV's modular Emerald SDK libraries hence creating a new Ethereum Classic client. The integrated marketplace allows programmers and non-programmers to be compensated for their work even when their component is used in another person's published application. Thus the user has an all-in-one marketplace of commercially supported components, a cryptocurrency to pay for the components and a platform to compose, create, mix, match and run them on.

Token Purpose:

  • The Initial Coin Offering allows one to purchase FRACTAL coins which will be traded in the integrated marketplace.
  • A buyer is someone who is prepared to offer FRACTALS for desired software available in the integrated marketplace that runs on Fractalide.
  • A seller is someone who writes Fractalide compatible software and makes it available on the integrated marketplace and is prepared to accept FRACTALS in return.
  • FRACTALS may also be used to contract programmers to write custom components.
  • A component author will always be compensated upon every sale even if their component forms part of another programmer's component hierarchy.
  • Programmers write components, non-programmers may compose components and sell them in the integrated marketplace. This Rapid Application Development environment is designed for non-programmers to solve problems. So money doesn't just flow to programmers.
